Connecting with Metal 金
合谷
He Gu — Joining Valley
Large Intestine 4 · LI-4

Dorsum of hand · between 1st & 2nd metacarpal

Location

In the webbing between the thumb and index finger, at the midpoint of the 2nd metacarpal bone on the back of the hand.

Metal Element Connection

He Gu opens the Lung and Large Intestine — the organs of receiving and releasing. Pressing here supports letting go of grief, clearing the lungs, and sharpening clarity.

  • 1
    Relax the right hand, palm facing down
  • 2
    Place left thumb on the web between thumb and index finger
  • 3
    Press gently inward toward the 2nd metacarpal bone
  • 4
    Hold and breathe slowly — exhale grief, inhale clarity
  • 5
    Repeat on the left hand for balance
Connecting with Earth 土
踏步
Marching
Activating Earth Energy · Spleen & Stomach Meridians

Lift knees · activate the center · feel the ground

The Practice

Marching in place activates the Stomach and Spleen meridians running along the front and inner legs. Earth governs digestion, nourishment, and our capacity to receive.

Earth Element Connection

Lifting and placing the foot with intention connects us to the ground — the source of Earth Qi. The steady rhythm quiets overthinking and returns us to center.

  • 1
    Stand feet shoulder-width apart, arms soft at sides
  • 2
    Slowly lift one knee — feel the inner thigh (Spleen meridian) engage
  • 3
    Place the foot down mindfully — heel first, then full sole
  • 4
    Alternate legs in a slow, steady rhythm — 36 steps total
  • 5
    Keep belly soft, lower abdomen relaxed, mind settled at center
