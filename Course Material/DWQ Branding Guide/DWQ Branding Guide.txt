The purpose of this brand guideline
To provide a comprehensive and detailed framework for
representing the Dragon’s Way Qigong® brand consistently
across all touchpoints. This document will serve as the primary
reference for anyone involved in creating or distributing branded
materials, ensuring that our brand identity remains coherent and
recognizable.
Use the guidelines and assets provided here and in your
Dragon’s Way Instructor materials. Do not create new items
using any portion of these materials and resources.
Stay in the circle of Qi, stand proudly with all Dragon’s Way
Qigong® Instructors, and receive the benefits in your life.
02
Our Mission
As Certified Dragonʼs Way Qigong®
Instructors, we carry an incredible gift that
can support and guide others to experience
and understand Qi, help them move towards
greater balance and calm, and discover the
power and answers that lie within. Through
this practice, they will be able to improve their
overall well-being.
Our vision is to connect with individuals who are
passionate about improving their health and
lives, both on an individual level and as part of
the global community. As a result, our practice
must consistently uphold the integrity of our
teachings and how we communicate them,
verbally, visually and energetically to people
and our communities.

CMYK Print Color
CMYK stands for cyan, magenta, yellow
color inks and is primarily used for
printed materials.
RGB Screen Color
RGB stands for red, green, and blue. This
color model is primarily used in digital
screen bases applications.
