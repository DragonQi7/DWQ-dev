## 4. Practical playbooks (step-by-step)

Playbook A — Conservative (capital preservation):
1. On signals of market topping / strong froth: sell 70–90% of crypto positions into USD (not stablecoins preferred by author).
2. Hold USD in insured bank or custodial USD accounts (Caleb & Brown, Uptrade.au noted in report) until BTC buy triggers.
3. When BTC ≤ $79K begin staged DCA into BTC until momentum returns and BTC holds above $90K.

Playbook B — Growth (balanced):
1. Sell 50–70% of riskier holdings at pre-defined sell targets (use tiered sell rules: 30% at 65% of target, next 30% at 75%, last 30% at 85%).
2. Keep 10% as "just in case" bag for extreme upside.
3. Hold USD for BTC DCA window; after BTC accumulation, selectively DCA into Stage 3 large caps and Stage 4 asymmetric picks during bear consolidation.

Playbook C — Aggressive (speculative)
1. Reduce leverage to zero immediately (report warns leverage is the #1 near-term risk).
2. Keep strict stop-loss discipline and position-size limits per coin (max 2–5% portfolio per high-risk microcap).
3. Take profit frequently (e.g., half at quick 2x) and scale out per targets.

Risk-control checklist (operational):
- Eliminate exchange leverage; avoid futures & margin unless you fully understand forced liquidation risk.
- Use cold storage for long-term holdings; split custody (do not keep all funds on a single exchange).
- Use limit orders for buys during volatility; set staggered limit buys for DCA ranges.
- Document every trade in a transaction log (template below).
