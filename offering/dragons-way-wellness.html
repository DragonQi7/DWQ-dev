About Dragon's Way Qigong®

Ancient wisdom.
Whole-person healing.

Dragon's Way Qigong® is a 6-week program developed by Grand Master Nan Lu of the TCM World Foundation, rooted in over 3,000 years of Taoist philosophy and Traditional Chinese Medicine. It is not exercise — it is energy cultivation, designed to restore the balance that chronic stress, modern living, and emotional strain have quietly taken away.

Through ten flowing movements, evidence-informed teaching, and Eating-for-Healing nutrition guidance, participants rebuild their Qi — their life force — from the inside out. The Five Element framework illuminates the connection between physical symptoms and emotional patterns, giving each person a language for their own healing.

What makes Dragon's Way Qigong® singular is its insistence on community. Weekly group discussion is never skipped — because real transformation happens in the presence of others who are also doing the work. This is how it has always been practiced, and why the results go far beyond what any solo effort can sustain.

This program is offered at [Location] — a thoughtfully created in-person space that shares Dragon's Way's commitment to whole-person well-being. Grounded, inclusive, and built for real connection, it is exactly the kind of setting where this practice comes alive.

Session Structure

How Each 90-Minute
Session is Built

Every session follows a consistent structure that builds skill, deepens understanding, and fosters community. This is not a workout — it is energy cultivation in practice and in conversation.

01

Qigong Movement Practice

Learn and practice Dragon's Way movements — introducing 1–2 new movements each week and reviewing the growing sequence. Movements build progressively across all 6 weeks until the full 10-movement sequence is complete.

30 Minutes
02

Course Material & Teaching

Explore the Five Element framework, organ-meridian connections, emotional patterns, and Eating-for-Healing principles. Each week's teaching directly supports understanding the element of that week.

30 Minutes
03

Group Discussion & Q&A

Share observations, ask questions, and grow in community. This is where the program becomes personal — guided prompts connect your life experience to that week's element and teaching. Never skipped.
