## The Body Knows What Season It Is

Your organs don't follow a calendar — they follow nature.

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, each season corresponds to specific organ systems, emotions, and energetic patterns. When we eat, sleep, and move *with* the season rather than against it, we use far less energy to feel well.

This issue: three herbs your body will welcome right now.

---

## Herb #1 — Astragalus (黄芪 Huáng Qí)

**What it does:** Strengthens Wei Qi — your body's defensive energy layer. Think of it as a daily upgrade for your immune system.

**How to use it:**
- Add dried root slices to soups and broths (remove before eating)
- Simmer 20 minutes in hot water as a mild, earthy tea
- Available in capsule form at most health stores

**Dose:** 9–30g of dried root daily is traditional. Start with a small amount and see how your body responds.

---

## Herb #2 — Schisandra Berry (五味子 Wǔ Wèi Zǐ)

Schisandra is called the "five flavor fruit" because it hits all five taste categories at once — sour, bitter, sweet, spicy, salty. In TCM, that breadth of flavor means it nourishes all five organ systems simultaneously.

**What it does:** Supports liver detoxification, calms the spirit, and has been used for centuries to support mental clarity and stamina.

**How to use it:** Dried berries in a decoction, or powdered in warm water with a little honey.

> *"The greatest medicine of all is teaching people how not to need it."* — Hippocrates

## Herb #3 — Goji Berry (枸杞子 Gǒu Qǐ Zǐ)

You've seen these in smoothie bowls. What most people don't know is that in classical texts, Goji is classified as a Jing (essence) tonic — meaning it's used to replenish the deepest reserves of vitality.

**What it does:** Nourishes Liver and Kidney Yin, supports eye health, and is rich in zeaxanthin and antioxidants.

**How to use it:** Eat a small handful raw as a snack. Or add to warm congee (rice porridge) in the morning.

## This Month's Practice — 5-Minute Morning Tea Ritual

Before coffee, before email — heat water to just below boiling and steep one of the three herbs above for 10 minutes. Sit with it. No screens.

This isn't about the herb. It's about giving your nervous system 5 minutes before the day takes over.

Try it for 7 days and notice.
