Understanding Your Qi

Why can’t you react calmly?
Why do you get so frustrated?

It didn’t happen overnight.

It took many years for your body to learn how to react this way. When you want to stay calm but can’t, it’s because you need to look at it from your body’s perspective. Over time, you trained your energy to want things urgently — this, that, everything at once. You told your body that if you don’t rush, you’ll miss it. If you don’t act right now, the moment will pass.

Your body learned that lesson well. And now it can’t stop.

The path you’re on — if nothing changes — will keep producing the same result. The way forward is to understand why your body is reacting this way. One powerful framework for that is the Five Elements. In that map, green represents the smooth flow of energy through the body.

The more Qi you have, the more body intelligence you have to handle difficult situations.

The less Qi you have, the more symptoms arise when life gets hard.

That’s why your world gets smaller. When Qi falls, so does everything built on top of it — your ability to handle stress, your finances, your relationships, your joy. It doesn’t happen all at once. It happens gradually, as the fuel runs lower.
