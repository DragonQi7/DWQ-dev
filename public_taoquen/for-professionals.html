The Burnout Cycle

Why your body keeps borrowing from tomorrow

Modern work runs on a feedback loop your nervous system was not designed for. Once you understand it, you can start to step out of it — one practice at a time.

01

Overdrive

Long hours, constant context-switching, stimulants. The body burns Qi faster than it can rebuild it.

02

Borrowing

When daily Qi runs out, the body pulls from deep reserves. You feel "fine" but you are spending capital.

03

Compensation

Anxiety rises, sleep degrades, body tension locks in. You compensate with more caffeine, more screens, less rest.

04

Collapse

A specific symptom appears — chronic insomnia, autoimmune flare, panic attacks. The bill comes due.
