Not all burnout is the same.

TCM describes three different patterns of depletion. Each one feels different — and each one has a different reset. Which one are you in right now?

Type 1

“I’m bone-tired and low-key scared about everything.”

Reset

Slow navel breathing. Hydrate. Consciously soften your jaw and shoulders. Think: soft, flexible, low pressure.

Type 2

“I’m wired and wound up — but I can’t release any of it.”

Reset

Move it out. Walk fast, shake out your arms and legs, stretch sideways through your ribs and hips. Emotions need somewhere to go.

TCM calls this: Wood →
Type 3

“I can’t stop thinking. My brain won’t land anywhere.”

Reset

Ground first. Eat something warm. Sit, breathe, feel your feet on the floor. Pick one task only — nothing else.

What to eat for your type

shows up in what your body craves.

TCM maps specific foods to specific patterns. Once you know your type, you know what to reach for.

💧 Bone-tired & anxious · Water

Build your reserves back

  • Black beans
  • Walnuts
  • Sesame seeds
  • Warm bone broth

Salty, dark, warming foods rebuild the body’s deepest reserves. Eat warm. Eat regularly. Avoid cold drinks.

🌿 Wired & stuck · Wood

Help your body release

  • Blueberries
  • Mung beans
  • Lemon water
  • Leafy greens

Sour foods help the body release what it’s holding. Light, fresh, green — let the energy move.

🌾 Overthinking & unsettled · Earth

Ground the system first

  • Warm ginger tea
  • Sweet potato
  • Oats or millet
  • Pumpkin

Regular, warm meals ground an overthinking system. Eat before your big study blocks — not during.
