Chinese Culture is a Qi Culture
Qi (say: "chee" ✨) is the life energy that flows through every living thing —
plants, animals, and YOU!

Ancient Chinese wisdom teaches us that this energy moves through
Five Elements — each one a different quality of Qi in nature and in us.

🌈 How Do YOU Connect to the 5 Elements?
Element What it means Try this every day! Secret body tip ✨
🌿 木 Wood
Wood energy helps you grow, plan, and feel excited about life. When Wood is balanced, you feel creative and free! Stretch your arms up tall like a tree 🌳 · Run and jump · Go outside in spring · Draw or make something new Eyes need rest 👁️ · Sleep before midnight
🔥 火 Fire
Fire energy brings joy, warmth, and connection. When Fire is balanced, you love being with people and feel happy inside! Laugh out loud 😂 · Dance to your favourite song · Hug a friend · Do something kind for someone Heart loves joy ❤️ · Say something kind today
🌍 土 Earth
Earth energy gives you stability, nourishment, and a feeling of home. When Earth is balanced, your tummy feels good and you feel safe! March slowly in place — heel first, 36 steps 🚶 · Eat without rushing · Hug someone you love Tummy tip 🌾 · Chew food slowly · Press your belly button gently
✨ 金 Metal
Metal energy helps you let go, breathe, and feel clear. When Metal is balanced, you can release worries and feel fresh inside! Take 3 deep breaths 💨 · Press He Gu (web between thumb & index finger) · Tidy your room · Look at the sky He Gu 合谷 ✋ · Press the web of your hand for 30 sec
💧 水 Water
Water energy brings wisdom, rest, and courage. When Water is balanced, you feel calm even when things are scary, and you love learning! Rest and be still 🌙 · Drink water · Close your eyes and breathe · Read a book quietly before bed Kidney loves warmth 🫘 · Keep lower back warm in winter
Remember: You have ALL 5 elements inside you — they work together like a team! 🌟
