📊 Trip Verdict & Budget
Trip Verdict
What's working, and what to fix before booking.
✅ What Works
🚗NYC → Grand Canyon Railway → desert road trip → San Diego is a classic, geographically sensible Americana route.
🍶Flushing before the China leg is a nice thematic bridge, and Great Wall + Terracotta Warriors + Panda Base covers the marquee highlight of each city without over-scheduling any single stop.
🎉July 4th fireworks plus a Manhattan museum/park/Chinatown day is an efficient, low-cost NYC intro for visiting cousins.
🏘️Shanghai is a strong closing stop — the Bund/skyline contrast with everything earlier in the trip, and it has direct flights home to Newark, fixing the "no return flight booked" gap from the original plan.
📝 Recommendations
😴Add one true rest day right after landing in Beijing, before the Great Wall trip — the 15-hour time change hits hard, and now there's an equally long haul (Shanghai→Newark) at the very end too.
🚗Split the Grand Canyon → San Diego drive over two days, or fly Phoenix/Flagstaff → San Diego and treat Joshua Tree as a separate trip.
🛑Book the Grand Canyon Railway one-way, the Panda Base tickets, and both Shanghai departure flights (family to Newark, cousins to Hong Kong) early — the group splits onto separate itineraries at the very end (already reflected above).
📅This is now genuinely a ~24-day trip, closer to 3.5 weeks than two. The Amtrak swap alone adds ~3 days over flying; if two weeks is a hard limit, the cleanest cuts are flying instead of training to Arizona, and/or dropping Chengdu/Shanghai or the Grand Canyon/Joshua Tree/San Diego leg.
🧣 General Tips Book the Amtrak legs, Grand Canyon Railway, Great Wall tour, Panda Base tickets, one-way rental car & all international flights well in advance. · July is peak season for all these routes — fares run higher than shoulder-season pricing. · The train doesn't carry cars — plan the rental car pickup for the South Rim, not Williams. · Two overnight Amtrak legs back to back is a lot — the Williams rest day helps, but consider a roomette instead of coach if budget allows. · The group splits in Shanghai on the last day: family flies direct to Newark, cousins fly on to Hong Kong separately — book both well ahead.
